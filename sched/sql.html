About Information Management (SQL)

This is a course that focuses on the fundamentals of Structured Query Language (SQL) and its application in managing data. SQL is a standard language used for storing, manipulating, and retrieving data in databases1. It is widely used in various fields such as data science, analytics, and engineering2. By learning SQL, you will gain the skills to work with large datasets
